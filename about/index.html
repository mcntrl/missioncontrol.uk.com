About Mission Control

Pioneering Digital Excellence Since 2013

At Mission Control, we’ve been crafting revolutionary interfaces and digital solutions for some of the biggest names in business for over a decade. From our inception in 2013, we set out with a clear mission: to transform how businesses and their customers experience the digital world.

Our Approach

We believe that exceptional digital experiences don’t happen by accident. They’re engineered through a careful balance of innovative thinking, technical expertise, and deep understanding of human behaviour. This philosophy has guided our approach to every project, whether we’re developing cutting-edge mobile applications, designing AI-powered tools, or creating Web3 solutions that push the boundaries of what’s possible.

Our work spans industries and continents, but the thread that connects it all is our commitment to building digital solutions that don’t just meet expectationsthey redefine them.

Our Heritage

Today, we're proud to call Northampton's vibrant Cultural Quarter our home. Our studio is housed within Vulcan Works, a Grade II listed building that stands as a testament to Britain's industrial heritage. Originally part of the 19th Century Vulcan Iron Works, these historic walls once echoed with the sounds of industrial innovationnow, they witness the digital revolution we're helping to shape.

There's something poetic about developing tomorrow's digital solutions in a space that once drove the industrial age forward. It's a daily reminder of the transformative power of innovation, and it inspires us to continue pushing boundaries in everything we do.
